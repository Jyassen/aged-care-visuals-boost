## "3 Questions That Could Save You Thousands on Medicare"

---

## **VIDEO SPECS:**
- **Duration:** 60 seconds
- **Format:** Faceless (text overlays + b-roll)
- **Target:** Adults 60-70 in Nassau/Suffolk County
- **CTA:** Comment "GUIDE" or book appointment

---

## **SCRIPT:**

### **[0-7 seconds] - HOOK**
**VISUAL:** Medicare card + money bills blurred in background  
**TEXT OVERLAY:** "3 Questions to Ask Your Medicare Agent"  
**VOICEOVER:**  
*"Before you sign up for any Medicare plan, ask your agent these 3 questions. Question 1..."*

---

### **[8-22 seconds] - QUESTION 1**
**VISUAL:** Split screen - doctor visit + prescription bottle + specialist office  
**TEXT OVERLAY:** 
- "Question #1"
- "What are my important copays?"

**VOICEOVER:**  
*"What are my important copays? Specifically: How much for my primary care doctor? My specialists? And my prescriptions? These three costs add up fast, and many plans hide the real numbers until it's too late."*

---

### **[23-40 seconds] - QUESTION 2**
**VISUAL:** Stethoscope + doctor's office + network diagram animation  
**TEXT OVERLAY:** 
- "Question #2"
- "Are MY doctors covered?"

**VOICEOVER:**  
*"Are my doctors covered in this plan? And do I need a referral to see them? There's nothing worse than switching plans and losing the doctors you trust. Get this in writing before you enroll."*

---

### **[41-52 seconds] - QUESTION 3**
**VISUAL:** Calculator animation + bill statements  
**TEXT OVERLAY:** 
- "Question #3"  
- "Do I have a deductible?"

**VOICEOVER:**  
*"Do I have a deductible? Both medical AND drug deductibles? Some plans advertise zero premium but hit you with thousands in deductibles. Know your total out-of-pocket before you commit."*

**Voice Talent:**
- Warm, trustworthy, conversational tone
- Pacing: Moderate (not rushed, but energetic)
- Gender: Either (test both)
- Age sound: 45-60 (experienced, not elderly)

**Music:**
- Upbeat but professional
- No distracting lyrics
- Volume: Low (20-30% of voiceover)
- Style: Corporate inspirational or light acoustic

**Color Grading:**
- Bright and clean
- Match YourMedGuy brand colors where possible
- Professional but approachable feel

**Pacing:**
- Quick cuts maintain attention
- Allow 0.5s pause between questions
