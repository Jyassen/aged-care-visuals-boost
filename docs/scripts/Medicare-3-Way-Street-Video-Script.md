## **LEGAL/COMPLIANCE CONSIDERATIONS:**

**Disclaimer to Include (in description or end card):**
*"This is educational content only. Medicare plan availability and costs vary by location. For personalized guidance, consult a licensed insurance agent. We represent multiple insurance carriers."*

**Avoid Saying:**
- "This path is best" (too definitive)
- "You should choose..." (advice without knowing situation)
- "Advantage plans are bad" (appears biased)
- Specific carrier names (remain independent)

**Safe Language:**
- "Which path fits your situation"
- "Many people prefer... others choose..."
- "Depends on your specific needs"
- "Let's review your options together"
